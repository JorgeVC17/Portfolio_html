Rat detector (Free Schedule Project)

I love learning about different machine learning techniques. That’s why I develop a Computer vision model that reconigze rats. Because I love the concept behind computer vision. It’s like teaching computers to recognize objects, understand scenes, and maybe even make decisions based on what they “see”. Rats are super important in Life Sciences for testing medicines, so being able to track them accurately is a big deal. Plus, understanding how they act is crucial for figuring out how medicines might affect us.
